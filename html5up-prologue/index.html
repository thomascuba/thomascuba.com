About Me

My name is Thomas Cuba. I am a 21 year old recent graduate from Rutgers University. I love computer science and maths of all forms, with a particular interest in cryptanalysis and game design theory.

My first language is English, but I am also a fluent native Slovak speaker, and have visited my other home country many times. My hope is to one day learn Slovak well enough to be able to speak in a professional business environment so that I may both assist Slovak immigrants and somehow help the ties between Slovakia and other nations around the world.
