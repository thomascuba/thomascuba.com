About Me

My name is Thomas Cuba. I am a 22 year old recent graduate from Rutgers University. I love computer science and maths of all forms, with a particular interest in cryptanalysis and game design theory.

My first language is English, but I am also a fluent native Slovak speaker, and have visited my other home country many times. My hope is to one day learn Slovak well enough to be able to speak in a professional business environment so that I may both assist Slovak immigrants and somehow help the ties between Slovakia and other nations around the world.

I pride myself as someone who holds an approximate knowledge of many things, and am always looking to add to said list of things. If you have anything interesting to tell me, feel free to.
